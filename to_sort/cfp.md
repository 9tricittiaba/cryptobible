- Title: 

How To Audit Applied Crypto

- Abstract: 

After having spent years reviewing cryptographic applications, best practices and patterns seem to emerge. This talk is a condense of what one should look for when looking for bugs in an application's usage of cryptography. From Encrypting communications to generating randomness, a lot can go wrong. What's the difference between all these padding oracle attacks? How do I hash passwords? What elliptic curve should I use?
